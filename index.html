WHY PHOTOGRAPHY?

Capturing moments, telling stories.

Photography is more than just taking pictures; it is the art of freezing time, capturing emotions, and telling stories through a single frame. Whether it’s a fleeting smile, a breathtaking landscape, or the play of light and shadow, photography allows us to preserve memories and see the world from a unique perspective.

Wether if I choose digital or analog, photography is for me an expression of creativity and passion. It challenges me to look beyond the ordinary, to find beauty in unexpected places, and to create images that evoke emotions and inspire others.

My favourite places.

Greece.

Offers a perfect mix of ancient history, breathtaking islands, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine. Wether you’re exploring ruins or relaxing by the sea, it’s an unforgettable destination.

Germany.

Where history and modern culture collide, it blends stunning landscapes and vibrant cities, like Berlin. From medieval castles to Oktoberfest celebrations, it offers a unique mix of culture and tradition.

Ireland.

Captivates with its stunning landscapes, from the Cliffs of Moher to the rolling green countryside. Its rich history, lively pubs, and warm hospitality make it a magical destination.

Italy.

A perfect blend of history, art, culture and scenic beauty, from Carrara's renowned marble quarries to Amalfi Coast's picturesque villages. Full of charming towns, vineyards, and a deep appreciation for la dolce vita, it’s a place to truly unwind and explore.

Morocco.

With its diverse culture, this country offers a mesmerizing mix of history and extraordinary nature, from the snow-capped Atlas Mountains to the labyrinthine alleys of Fez. Marrakesh’s lively markets, filled with spices and artisans, make it a sensory journey unlike any other.

Hungary.

Rich in traditions, stunning architecture, and a deep love for music and cuisine. It captivates with its grand architecture, like the stunning Parliament Building. Famous for its thermal baths and lively ruin bars in Budapest, you will find both fun and relaxation in the same place.
